## Background

On July 28, 2006, actor and director Mel Gibson was arrested for driving under the influence in Malibu, California. During the arrest, Gibson made antisemitic statements to the arresting officers, which were documented in the police report and later became public, creating significant controversy in Hollywood and beyond.

## The Incident

### Arrest Details
Gibson was stopped by Los Angeles County Sheriff's Deputy James Mee for speeding on Pacific Coast Highway. His blood alcohol level was measured at 0.12%, above California's legal limit of 0.08%.

### Documented Statements
According to the official police report, Gibson made several antisemitic remarks during the arrest, including blaming Jewish people for "all the wars in the world" and making derogatory comments about Jewish people in the entertainment industry.

### Initial Response
Gibson initially attempted to have the antisemitic comments removed from the police report, but the statements had already been documented and would later become public through media reporting.

## Public Disclosure and Media Coverage

### TMZ Report
TMZ was the first media outlet to report on the antisemitic statements, publishing details from the arrest report that had been leaked to them. This marked the beginning of widespread media coverage.

### Mainstream Media Response
Major news outlets including CNN, ABC News, The New York Times, and The Washington Post covered the story extensively, focusing on both the DUI arrest and the antisemitic statements.

### Entertainment Industry Coverage
Trade publications like Variety, The Hollywood Reporter, and Entertainment Weekly examined the potential impact on Gibson's career and ongoing projects.

## Immediate Responses and Consequences

### Gibson's Public Apology
Gibson issued multiple public apologies, including a statement to the media and later a more detailed apology specifically to the Jewish community. He acknowledged that his statements were "despicable" and took responsibility for his actions.

### Anti-Defamation League Response
The ADL initially rejected Gibson's first apology as insufficient, calling for more concrete actions to demonstrate genuine remorse. They later engaged with Gibson on educational initiatives.

### Industry Reactions
**Talent Agencies**: Some agents and managers distanced themselves from Gibson following the incident.

**Studio Executives**: Several Hollywood executives privately expressed concerns about working with Gibson on future projects.

**Fellow Actors**: Various actors and directors made public statements either condemning Gibson's remarks or calling for forgiveness and rehabilitation.

## Legal Proceedings and Resolution

### DUI Charges
Gibson pleaded no contest to the DUI charges and was sentenced to three years probation, alcohol education classes, and community service.

### Civil Implications
While no civil lawsuits directly resulted from the antisemitic statements, the incident affected Gibson's public image and business relationships.

### Law Enforcement Response
The Los Angeles County Sheriff's Department faced questions about the initial handling of the arrest report and the leak of sensitive information to the media.

## Career and Professional Impact

### Immediate Projects
**Apocalypto (2006)**: Gibson's directorial project was already in post-production and was released as scheduled, though it faced additional scrutiny due to the controversy.

**Acting Roles**: Several planned acting projects were cancelled or postponed following the incident.

### Long-term Career Effects
**Directorial Work**: Gibson's opportunities as a director were significantly reduced, with major studios becoming reluctant to finance his projects.

**Acting Opportunities**: His acting career was substantially impacted, with fewer leading roles offered by major studios.

**Independent Projects**: Gibson increasingly turned to independent financing for his subsequent directorial efforts.

## Rehabilitation Efforts and Public Response

### Educational Initiatives
Gibson participated in educational programs about antisemitism and Holocaust awareness, working with Jewish organizations to demonstrate his commitment to making amends.

### Charitable Contributions
He made donations to various Jewish charities and Holocaust education organizations as part of his efforts to repair relationships with the Jewish community.

### Continued Controversy
Despite rehabilitation efforts, Gibson faced ongoing scrutiny and criticism, with some in the entertainment industry remaining unwilling to work with him.

## Industry and Cultural Impact

### Hollywood's Response to Antisemitism
The incident prompted broader discussions within the entertainment industry about antisemitism and the consequences for public figures who make such statements.

### Media Coverage Standards
The case raised questions about media responsibility in reporting on celebrity arrests and the balance between public interest and privacy.

### Rehabilitation and Forgiveness
Gibson's case became a reference point for discussions about whether and how public figures can rehabilitate their careers after making antisemitic statements.

## Long-term Consequences and Current Status

### Career Recovery Attempts
Gibson has continued to work in the entertainment industry, though primarily in independent films and with significantly reduced mainstream opportunities compared to his pre-2006 career.

### Ongoing Scrutiny
New projects involving Gibson continue to face scrutiny and controversy, with some actors and industry professionals refusing to work with him.

### Cultural Legacy
The incident remains a significant part of Gibson's public legacy and continues to be referenced in discussions about antisemitism in Hollywood.
